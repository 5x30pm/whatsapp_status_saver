WhatsApp Status Saver

Learn how to save WhatsApp status videos and photos with these easy methods!

📱Method 1: Using File Manager (Android)

  1. Watch Whatsapp/ WA-Business Status Onace
  2. Open your phone’s File Manager (like Files by Google or your default one).
  3. Navigate to:
    For Whatsapp-->Internal Storage > Android > media > com.whatsapp > WhatsApp > Media > .Statuses


For WA-Business-->Internal Storage > Android > media > com.whatsapp.w4b > WhatsApp Business > Media > .Statuses


  1. If you don’t see the .Statuses folder:
  2. Tap the three dots (menu) in the File Manager and select “Show hidden files” or “Show system files.”
  1. Open the .Statuses folder to view all the status videos and images you’ve seen.
  2. Tap and hold on any video or image to select it, then choose “Copy” or “Move.”
  3. Navigate to a different folder (like “Downloads” or “Pictures”) and paste the copied/moved status media there,, otherwise they’ll disappear after 24 hours.
  1. You can now view the saved status media in your gallery or file manager.



📱Method 2: Using WhatsApp Web (for videos/photos)

  1. Open WhatsApp Web on your PC or laptop.
  2. Scan the QR code with your phone’s WhatsApp to log in.
  3. Click on the “Status” tab on the left sidebar.
  4. Click on any status you want to save to open it.
  5. Right-click on the video or photo and select “Save video as…” or “Save image as…” to download it to your computer.



📱Method 3: Screen Recording / Screenshot (for videos/photos)

  • For photos → take a screenshot
  • For videos → use built-in screen recorder

    (Most Android phones already have this feature)
